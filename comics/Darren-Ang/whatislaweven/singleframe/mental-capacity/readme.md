**Mental capacity** isn't just about permanent intellectual disabilities; you might be mentally incapable if you're passed out for a long time, or if you have this condition which triggers from time to time, or even if you're unable to communicate your desires at all. But generally, you can't decide for mentally incapable people unless you get some legal powers in advance.
